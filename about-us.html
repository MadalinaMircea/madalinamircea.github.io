How we got started

Unicorn Publicies was born in 2016, more out of necessity than anything else. The company owner, Maddie, was already working as a freelancer, but she could not issue invoices, sign contracts... basically, she was flying under the radar. As soon as she turned 18 and could legally open her own business, that's exactly what she did!

What and how

We worked on video editing for the first 4 years. We perfected our skills in different editing software, finally settling on Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and various whiteboard applications, like VideoScribe. 

We slowly started doing more design work, illustrations, 3D models, and websites. We use Photoshop for most 2D tasks. 3D models are usually created using Maya, but we are transitioning to ZBrush and Cinema4D. We use wordpress and custom code for websites.

Getting into games

Our O.U. (i.e. Original Unicorn) started developing games in college, as part of her school projects. She continued to study games for her dissertation during the first Master's Degree she enrolled in, even though the college she was attending at the time was not very fond of game development. She is currently enrolled in her second Master's Degree, specializing in Digital Media and Game Studies.

We develop games into Unity and are looking to branch out towards Unreal. We write code in C# and use custom models or flipped assets.

What's next?

We are currently developing our first full release game. We are working on 3 more ideas, ranging from mobile to PC and console, and from casual to hardcore. We are confident that within the next few years, every gamer out there will find something to enjoy in our portfolio!

Don't forget to subscribe to our newsletter to be the first to know when our full releases are out!
